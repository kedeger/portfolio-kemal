Who am I?

A full stack web developer with marketing skills and business mind. My path to web development actually began with marketing – when a two-year high school digital marketing internship turned into a web marketing consultant gig servicing natural health clients such as Bastyr University, NDHealthcare.com and AANMC.org. To solidify my knowledge I enrolled in General Assembly’s 10-week part-time Digital Marketing course. Upon completing the course, I had been around GA long enough to see the importance of understanding computing languages in our society, and decided to dive head first into their Web Development Immersive program, which has taught me a wide range of applications for different technologies, but most importantly – what it means to be a flexible developer that can adapt to new languages and frameworks in a small amount of time.

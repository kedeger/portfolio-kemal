About me

A full stack web developer combined with a digital marketer. For me, web development began with a two year marketing internship I worked in high school that turned into a web marketing consultant gig servicing natural health clients such as Bastyr University, NDHealthcare.com and AANMC.org. To solidify my marketing knowledge I enrolled in General Assembly’s 10-week Digital Marketing course. The deeper I got into Digital Marketing, the more it became apparent how useful and important it is to develop the capacity to work with hard code. I decided to dive head first into GA's 3 month intensive Web Development Immersive program, which taught me how to build full stack web applications using a wide range of frameworks and technologies, but most importantly – how to be a flexible developer that can adapt to new languages and frameworks on the fly. After graduating the course, I was hired on at Bastyr University as a contractor to assist in switching to their new website earler this year, as well as manage the old website bug fixes and setup email marketing campaigns.
